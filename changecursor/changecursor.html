To change look & theme of cursor free

Let's start

Click on google chrome and then search sweezy cursors.

Then select a cursor and click on download and select the complete pack.png.
Then right click and extract files.
Then open control panel.
Select Large catagory.
Click on mouse.
Click on pointers option.
Click on browse.
Then find and select the file you extracted.
Click on open.
Click on apply and then click on ok.
