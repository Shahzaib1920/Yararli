Change language in win 7

To change and set any language in window 7 for free

Let Start!

Open start menu and search control panel.

Set view by large icon.
Click on Region and Language.
Then click on keyboards and languages bar.
Then click on Change keyboards.
Then click on add.
Then click language you want to add such I want to add English Language and country United States, so I click on "+"icon of English(United States).
Then I click on "+" icon of keyboard and click on United States-Dovrak.
Then click on okay and next you known, that click on apply and okay.
Done
